Illuminae

A sci-fi story told through hacked documents, chats, and schematics.

I LOVE the way it mixes narrative formats. Normal books are boring asf now. But Illuminae, and the other two books in the series, mix notes, emails, and security footage. It just makes for such an interesting story. First book I've ever cried about. Can you guess the other two?

Gemina

Another mind-bending chapter in the Illuminae Files with new protagonists.

The pacing is insane. I picked it up and literally couldn't put it down. Even though I've read it like 600 times, I always forget about Nik and Hanna dying with each other in their alternate universes and start bawling. Even if I remembered, I would still be bawling lmao.

Obsidio

The finale that ties everything together in an epic showdown.

High stakes, emotional punches, and so satisfying to see all storylines collide. Asha and Kady have the best dynamic, and I was so stressed reading this book. But the ending? Perfect. Them meeting up in the restaurant, and Nik bumming off of Hanna? Chef's kiss.
