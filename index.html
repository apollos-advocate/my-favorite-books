Illuminae

A sci-fi story told through hacked documents, chats, and schematics.

I LOVE the way it mixes narrative formats. Normal books are boring asf now. But Illuminae, and the other two books in the series, mix notes, emails, and security footage. It just makes for such an interesting story. First book I've ever cried about.

Gemina

Another mind-bending chapter in the Illuminae Files with new protagonists.

The pacing is insane. I picked it up and literally couldn't put it down. I could've sworn the book was glued to my fingertips. Even though I've read it like 600 times, I always forget about Nik and Hanna dying in their alternate universes and start bawling. Even if I remembered I would still be bawling lmao.
