Solane’s Favorite Books 📚

because I have very important opinions

Illuminae

A sci-fi story told through hacked documents, chats, and schematics.

I just LOVE the way it mixes narrative formats. Normal books are boring asf now. But Illuminae and the other two books mix notes, emails, and security footage. Only three books have ever made me cry, this is one of them. Can you guess the other two?

Gemina

Another mind-bending chapter in the Illuminae Files with new protagonists.

The pacing is insane. Even though I've read it like 600 times, I still bawl at Nik and Hanna’s scenes every single time. Alternate universe deaths? Yeah, that broke me. Shattered.

Obsidio

The finale that ties everything together in an epic showdown.

High stakes, emotional punches, and so satisfying to see all storylines collide. The ending made me feel like I earned it.
